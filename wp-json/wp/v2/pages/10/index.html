Smart Showers for a Curated Experience<\/strong><\/p>\n

A typical person will use about 17 gallons of water each time they take a shower. Low-flow shower heads help reduce water consumption however, new smart shower valves can lower the total volume of water even further. For instance, users may spend extra time trying to adjust the water temperature to their preferred setting. Smart valves can be voice-controlled to set the temperature to a specific degree therefore minimizing the temperature adjustment time. These valves use a stepper motor to precisely open and close each water input (hot and cold). This feature would be ideal in hotels, gyms and spas because people typically spend longer adjusting water temperature when using an unfamiliar faucet.<\/p>\n
